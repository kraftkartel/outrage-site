About Us

The Sound of Rwanda to the World

OutRage Music Records is more than a studio — it's a full creative hub rooted in Kigali, the beating heart of East Africa. We are the bridge between local artistry and global sound standards.

Founded on the belief that African stories deserve world-class production, we work with artists, brands, filmmakers, and content creators across the continent and beyond. From street-verified Kinyatrap anthems to cinematic dubbing and commercial voiceover — every project gets the OutRage treatment.

Our multilingual capabilities spanning East African and international languages make us the premier studio for any project targeting diverse African audiences.
